Campaigns facilitate communication with your users through various messaging channels. These include App Inbox, Email, In-App messages, Push notifications, SMS and WhatsApp.

### Creating a campaign

Creating a campaign includes the following steps
1. Capturing the WHAT : Campaign details
2. Capturing the WHY : Conversion goal details
3. Capturing the WHO : Audience to be targetted
4. Capturing the WHERE : Channel selected for the campaign
5. Capturing the WHEN : Campaign schedule & settings details
