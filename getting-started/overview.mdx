title: "Overview"
description: "This documentation will cover the following:"
---

1. **Setup Castled**: Setup involves configuring the data warehouse, creating
   the business entities and configuring the channels. Castled setup is covered
   in detail [here](/getting-started/castled-setup).
2. **Build Customer Segment**: Customer Segment is the backbone of Campaigns or
   Workflows run in Castled. Creating the segment is covered in detail
   [here](/getting-started/build-segment).
3. **Start a Campaign/Workflow**: Once the setup is complete and the segments
   are created, marketers can run campaigns or workflows targetting the leads in
   the selected segment. Refer [campaigns](/getting-started/run-campaign) and
   [workflows](/getting-started/create-workflow) for more details.
