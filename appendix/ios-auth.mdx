---
title: "iOS Authentication Key Setup"
---

### 1. Sign-in to [apple developer center](https://idmsa.apple.com/IDMSWebAuth/signin?appIdKey=891bd3417a7776362562d2197f89480a8547b108fd934911bcbea0110d07f757&path=%2Faccount%2F&rv=1).

### 2. Select the **Keys** option in Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles tab

![Key Config](/images/ios-auth-2.png)

### 3. Click on **Create**.

![Key Create](/images/ios-auth-3.png)

### 4. Give a name for the key. Also, enable Apple Push Notifications service (APNs)

![Key New](/images/ios-auth-4.png)

### 5. Click **Register**.

### 6. Download the key. Note down the **Key ID** which will be later used when configuring APNS in Castled.

![](/images/ios-auth-6.png)

### 7. `Team ID` can be obtained by going to the **Membership details** tab after logging into the apple developer account.

![](/images/ios-auth-7.png)

### 8. `Bundle ID` can be obtained from Xcode by navigating to **Signing & Capabilities** tab.
