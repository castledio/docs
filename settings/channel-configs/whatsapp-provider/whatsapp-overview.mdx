## Supported WhatsApp BSPs

Castled supports the following WhatsApp Service providers:

- [Twilio](/settings/channel-configs/whatsapp-provider/twilio)
- [Gupshup](/settings/channel-configs/whatsapp-provider/gupshup)
- [Karix](/settings/channel-configs/whatsapp-provider/karix)
- [MSG91](/settings/channel-configs/whatsapp-provider/msg91)
- [ValueFirst](/settings/channel-configs/whatsapp-provider/valuefirst)
- [Textlocal](/settings/channel-configs/whatsapp-provider/textlocal)
